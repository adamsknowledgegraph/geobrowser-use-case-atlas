Why knowledge graphs

Knowledge graphs turn scattered data into reusable context.

Most data is trapped in pages, spreadsheets, feeds, PDFs, and one-off apps. A knowledge graph organizes that information as connected objects: people, places, claims, events, sources, studies, companies, topics, and relationships. That structure makes the data easier to verify, compare, govern, and reuse.
