Why knowledge graphs

Knowledge graphs empower provenance, verifiability, and context.

Instead of losing information inside isolated pages, databases, PDFs, and apps, a knowledge graph keeps people, places, claims, sources, studies, events, and topics connected. That makes simple but important questions easier to answer: who said what, what is the context, and is it verified?
